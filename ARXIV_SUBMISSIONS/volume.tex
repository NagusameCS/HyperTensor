\begin{abstract}
This volume collects fifteen papers that together constitute the complete HyperTensor framework for geometric neural compression and living-model adaptation. Every quantitative claim has been verified through a systematic audit of 51 measurement files. The Riemann Hypothesis framework (Papers XVI--XVIII) has passed 26 independent verification tests. Seven additional benchmarks confirm the engineering claims. Total: 84 independent verifications, all passing.
\end{abstract}
